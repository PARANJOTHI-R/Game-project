ABOUT US

At Gametable, we design games that are easy to pick up and hard to put down. We aim for a broad appeal with our classic board, word, and pen & paper style games.

Our games always save progress securely in your browser's local storage so you can play for a bit, put it down, and return later where you left off without all the hassle of creating an account and remembering a password. We also design our website and games for the best possible experience on any device so you can play on your computer, tablet, or phone. All of this is crucial to our goal of providing casual game play fun!
