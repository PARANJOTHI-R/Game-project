How to Play:

The game is played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns placing their markers (X or O) in an empty cell.

The first player to get three of their markers in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.

If all cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
